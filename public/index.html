About us

Who We Are

Ami is a high school mentorship program, connecting experienced collegiate scholars with proactive high school students.

Our mentors don’t just teach you to navigate high school—they know that high school is a stepping stone for future personal growth. They teach you what works best and connect you with people and programs that will take you further. We understand that the last years before college are those of personal growth, so we pair mentors and mentees who share a high school experience.

Our Philosophy

Lifelong success can only be attained with distilled interests and cultivated passions. We are not tutors, and we are not college counselors. Our mission is to recharge the high school experience by focusing on what really matters: an unfettered faith in your ability to pursue.

Our Services

Discovery

High school is a medley of new stories and opportunities. Our mentors uncover what drives you, and push you in.

Academic Counseling

Academics should be source of enrichment and confidence, not stress. We provide guidance on how to discover expectations and succeed.

Extracurricular Enrichment

Our mentors connect you with internships, camps, and programs that will inspire you to transform your interests into a personal impact.

Networking

Recognize who can take you farther and higher. With teachers, professionals, and leaders, learn to develop empowered relationships for the future.

Emotional Support

Life and school are stressful. Those who are successful and capable do not avoid stress, they learn to wield it.
